# Interior Design Platform

## Project Summary

The Interior Design Platform is a web application that connects clients looking for interior design services with professional interior designers.

Clients can browse designer portfolios, explore completed projects, submit project requests, and communicate with designers through the platform.

Interior designers can create profiles, showcase their work, manage project enquiries, and receive new clients.

The system aims to simplify the process of finding trusted interior designers while providing designers with an online marketplace to grow their businesses.

---

## Objectives

- Connect clients with interior designers
- Display designer portfolios
- Allow clients to request quotations
- Allow designers to manage projects
- Provide a professional online marketplace

---

## Target Users

### Clients

People looking for:

- Home interior design
- Office design
- Kitchen remodeling
- Furniture consultation

### Designers

Professional interior designers who want to:
